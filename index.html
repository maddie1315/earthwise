Fast Facts:

  • 80+ billion items of clothing are purchased each year
  • 16 billion tons of clothing are thrown away annually in the US
  • 85% of discarded textiles go to the dump each year
  • The equivalent of one garbage truck of clothing is burned or dumped in a landfill every second
  • Fashion is the 2nd most polluting industry globally
  • 1.2 billion tons of CO2 are released by the fast fashion industry annually
  • 10% of annual global emissions come from the fashion industry
  • The clothing industry is the 2nd largest consumer and polluter of the world's water supply
